Firmware Updates

Outdated router firmware can cause disconnection issues. Here's how to update:

  1. Access router admin panel (usually http://192.168.1.1)
  2. Login with admin credentials
  3. Find "Firmware Update" or "System Update"
  4. Download and install latest version
  5. Restart router after update

Router Age Matters

If your router is more than 5 years old, consider upgrading. Older routers often have stability issues.

Frequently Asked Questions About internet keeps disconnecting

Why does my internet keep disconnecting every few minutes?

This is usually caused by router issues, ISP problems, or interference. Try restarting your router first, then check for service outages in your area.

Is it normal for internet to disconnect occasionally?

Occasional disconnections (once per week) can happen, but daily disconnections indicate a problem that needs fixing. Contact your ISP if basic troubleshooting doesn't help.

Should I contact my ISP if internet keeps disconnecting?

Yes, if you've tried basic troubleshooting (restart, check cables) and the issue persists for more than a day, contact your ISP to check for service issues.
