Common Questions

Why is my internet slow at night?

Internet speeds often slow down during peak hours (7-11 PM) when everyone in your neighborhood is streaming. This is called network congestion. Learn how to fix this ‚Üí

What's the difference between download and upload speed?

Download speed affects how fast you receive data (streaming, browsing). Upload speed affects how fast you send data (video calls, uploading). Most plans have higher download than upload speeds. Learn more ‚Üí

How accurate is this speed test?

Our test is highly accurate because it runs directly in your browser using reliable CDN endpoints. For best results, test with an Ethernet cable and close other applications. Learn about accuracy ‚Üí
